# Human Edge — Sales Diagnostic Tool

> **Purpose:** Expose the judgment gap in revenue terms. Executive-level entry point for workshops, audits, and proposals.

This tool sits upstream of the scoring rubric and coaching guide. Use it to diagnose an organization's Human Edge maturity before rolling out the program.

---

## 1. Diagnostic Questions

Use these as workshop discussion prompts or in a 1:1 executive audit.

### Customer Understanding
Can your team describe their **top 5 customers' business objectives** — not the products you sell them?

### Conversation Depth
What **% of deals** include an uncovered problem that was *not* in the RFP?

### Trust Signal
How often do customers **initiate contact** vs. only respond when you reach out?

### AI Usage Quality
Are AI outputs being:
- Used directly as-is, OR
- Challenged, refined, and improved by human judgment?

### Manager Behavior
Are managers coaching **pipeline** or **thinking**?

---

## 2. Organization Scoring

| Area | Low | Mid | High |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Thinking Capability** | Reactive | Inconsistent | Strategic |
| **Customer Insight** | Surface | Partial | Deep |
| **Trust Position** | Transactional | Mixed | Advisory |
| **AI Usage** | Output generation | Assisted thinking | Judgment amplification |

---

## 3. Revenue Impact Model

Tie the diagnostic directly to revenue to make the business case:

- **Win rate gap:** 18–22% (Replaceable) → 32–40% (Human Edge)
- **Lost deals per team:** 8–14 per quarter
- **Avg deal:** $180K–$400K

> **Output statement for the client:**
> "Your Human Edge gap is costing $X annually."

---

## 4. What the Client Receives

A diagnostic engagement produces:

1. **Current Quadrant Distribution** — what % of the sales force is Replaceable / Transitional / Human Edge
2. **Manager Effectiveness Score** — how well managers are coaching thinking vs. activity
3. **AI Risk Exposure** — which roles/motions are most vulnerable to replacement
4. **Revenue Leakage Estimate** — the $ cost of the current gap
5. **90-Day Action Plan** — specific behavior shifts, rubric deployment, coaching cadence

---

## 5. How the Three Pieces Fit Together

```
Diagnostic  →  Sells the need + proves ROI
Rubric      →  Measures behavior
Coaching    →  Changes behavior
```

Each one alone fails. Together they are the system.
