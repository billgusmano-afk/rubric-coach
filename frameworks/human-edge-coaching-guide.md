# Human Edge — Manager Coaching Guide

> **Core Principle:** Managers don't inspect activity — they upgrade thinking.

This guide pairs with the Human Edge Commercial scoring rubric. The rubric measures behavior. This guide changes it.

---

## 1. Replace These Questions

| Pipeline Jockey | Human Edge Coach |
|---|---|
| "Where is the deal?" | "What problem are they actually solving?" |
| "Did you send the proposal?" | "What objection haven't they told you?" |
| "Why did you lose?" | "What didn't you ask?" |

---

## 2. The 5 Coaching Moves

Train every manager on these — they are the working moves of a Human Edge coach.

### 1. Push One Layer Deeper
> "Why does that matter to them?"

### 2. Force Specificity
> "How do you know that's the problem?"

### 3. Challenge Assumptions
> "What if that's not the real issue?"

### 4. Translate to Business Impact
> "What happens if they do nothing?"

### 5. Reframe the Conversation
> "What question would change this deal?"

---

## 3. Weekly Coaching Cadence (Non-Negotiable)

**Every rep brings:**
- 1 real account
- 1 stuck deal
- 1 missed opportunity

**Manager does:**
1. Score the rep's thinking on that account using the Human Edge rubric.
2. Name the **thinking gap** — which of the 5 dimensions is weakest (Rigor, Relevance, Credibility, Intimacy, Self-Orientation)?
3. Assign the **next conversation behavior** — a specific, observable move to practice in the next customer touch.

---

## 4. The Output-Layer Question

After every scored session, require an answer to:

> **"What did this person understand that others would have missed?"**

If the honest answer is *nothing* → the rep is not in Human Edge territory, regardless of composite score.

---

## 5. Manager KPI Shift

**Measure:**
- Coaching frequency (sessions/week per rep)
- Conversation quality score (rubric composite)
- Deal progression *quality* (not just stage movement)

**Stop measuring in isolation:**
- Raw pipeline volume
- Activity counts (calls, emails, demos)
